Nutrition Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid excessive sugar and processed foods.

Exercise Tips

  • Engage in at least 30 minutes of physical activity every day.
  • Try different types of exercises, like cardio and strength training.
  • Stay consistent for better long-term health benefits.

Common Health Issues

Many people in the Alpha Generation face issues like obesity, poor mental health, and lack of physical activity. Developing healthy habits early on is essential.
